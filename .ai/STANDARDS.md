# Standards

## Equal-first

- Cursor, Claude, and Codex are treated as equals.
- No platform is primary for capability definition.
- All canonical skills, agents, and rules must map across all tools.

## Capability requirements

### Rules

Required fields: `id`, `intent`, `scope`, `requirements`, `anti_patterns`.

### Skills

Required fields: `id`, `name`, `description`, `when_to_use`, `workflow`, `inputs`, `outputs`, `references`.

### Agents

Required fields: `id`, `name`, `purpose`, `when_to_delegate`, `checklist`, `report_format`, `tool_limits`.
